How to Use LeadPro AI

LeadPro AI is designed to streamline your lead generation process. Here's how to get started:

  1. AI Generator: Use this feature to automatically generate leads based on your criteria.
  2. Find Contacts: Extract contact information from websites, emails, and more.
  3. Social Media Lookup: Analyze social media profiles to gather additional insights about your leads.
  4. Bulk Email Sender: Send mass emails to your leads, personalizing messages for better engagement.
  5. Email Validation: Validate email addresses to reduce bounce rates and increase deliverability.
  6. Phone Validation: Check phone numbers to ensure they are valid and active before contacting.
  7. Help Center: Access the Help Center for guidance and support on using LeadPro AI.

For more detailed instructions, visit our Help Center or contact support.
